Use a recipient address that is not already connected to your deposit wallet, exchange account, public profile, or earlier Nullark activity.

Avoid immediate deposit-then-withdraw patterns. Waiting for more same-denomination activity can reduce simple timing links.

:::risk_caveat
Fresh recipients, fixed denominations, and waiting periods are risk reductions. They are not guarantees of anonymity or unlinkability.
:::
