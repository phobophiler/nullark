---
title: "Deposit"
description: "A user-facing checklist for fixed-denomination deposits without exposing note material."
---

A deposit creates a public pool interaction and private note material. The deposit itself is public.

<Warning>
  Do not deposit from an address or timing pattern that you are unwilling to have publicly associated with this pool interaction.
</Warning>

## Before depositing

Check:

- the app origin is the expected Nullark origin
- the wallet is connected to MegaETH mainnet chain ID `4326`
- the pool address matches `public-artifacts/current.json`
- the denomination and fee expectations are clear
- you understand that the deposit transaction is public
- you have a private place to keep recovery material

## During deposit

The app creates note material locally and submits a pool deposit. Do not copy note material into chat, screenshots, issue trackers, support channels, or docs.

## After deposit

Keep the note and recovery boundary private. If the browser profile, wallet unlock flow, app origin, or recovery record is compromised, the private-balance boundary can fail even when the contract and proof are correct.

<Info>
  Waiting longer and avoiding obvious amount, timing, and recipient patterns can reduce avoidable links. It does not create a guarantee.
</Info>
