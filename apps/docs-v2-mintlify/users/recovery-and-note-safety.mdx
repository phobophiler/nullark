---
title: "Recovery and note safety"
description: "Recovery boundaries without exposing secrets or implying custody."
---

Nullark recovery is a local-user safety boundary, not a custody or support recovery service.

## What users must protect

Protect:

- wallet access
- note secrets
- recovery secrets
- browser profile data
- decrypted note material
- wallet unlock signatures

## What support cannot receive

Support, docs, and public issues cannot receive:

- seed phrases
- private keys
- raw note secrets
- recovery secrets
- raw witnesses
- decrypted note payloads
- unredacted screenshots containing wallet prompts or recovery material

## Support boundary

Support can help verify public runtime fields, contract addresses, artifact hashes, and visible error categories. Support cannot receive or reconstruct private note material.
